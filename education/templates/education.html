Morgan State University,

Bachelors Of Science, Electrical and Computer Engineering.
2013 - 2017

Morgan State University is an HBCU (Historically Black Colleges and Universities). The Electrical and Computer Engineering program is an accredited and well-known program in the United States. The program aims to help students not only with theoretical knowledge but also with practical experience. This is done with a Bachelors thesis which is to be presented to a panel in order to obtain an engineering degree. Apart from course-work, the engineering program offers a volunteering program with AMIE (Advancing Minorities Interests In Engineering).

The AMIE Tutorial Program is an after school tutoring program for middle and high school students from the Baltimore City Public School System. The program is designed to assist students in Mathematics and help cultivate an interest in science and engineering. The goal of the program is to increase the mathematics skill proficiency of middle school students in preparation for the Maryland State Mathematics Proficiency Examination and prepare high school students for the SAT. Morgan State University's engineering students tutor students Monday through Thursday evenings at the Clarence M. Mitchell School of Engineering. The eight-week program is offered in the fall and spring.

University of Oregon,

Masters Of Science, Product Management.
2017 - 2019

University of Oregon is the home of Nike. This is where the global brand, Nike was formed. The University of Oregon, Product Management program focuses on its students learning the complete life-cycle of products from Ideation to Go To Market. Professors from the Industry are called in to oversee seminars that will give students an insight into products. Every year, only 40 students are admitted to this prestigious program.

Students gain professional experience through a mandatory internship on the brand-side or at a global manufacturer. Furthermore, students gain international experience by attending ISPO, Germany and looking at brand marketing at different geo-markets such as China and Japan.
